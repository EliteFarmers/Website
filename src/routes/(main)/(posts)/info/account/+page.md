# Accounts

## Perks

Signing into the website with your Discord account unlocks the following perks:

### Account linking

- Your linked Minecraft accounts show a checkmark on their stats pages to show it's linked.
- Save some configured settings to your profiles on the fortune page.
- Apply cosmetics bought from the [shop](/shop) to your accounts.

If you want to link your account, please [login](/login) then go to [your profile](/profile)!

### Server Management

When you login to the website you'll gain access to manage the bot in any Discord servers you own or moderate!

> [!IMPORTANT]
> You must have **admin** perms or be the owner of the server to be able to configure it!

To do so, navigate to your [server list](/profile/servers) in your profile. If your server doesn't show up here yet
please be patient and make sure you've invited the bot! ([Invite Link](/invite))

Read more about server management [here](/info/servers)!

### Persisted Settings

Some data on the site is persisted when you're logged into an account!

- Dismissed Alerts
- Favorite Pages - **Coming Soon!**

### Join Events

If you're logged into an account you're able to join events! Keep an eye out on the sidebar for events!
You can also check out the [browse](/browse) page to see past events in servers.

## Cosmetics

You're able to manage cosmetics that you've bought or unlocked from the [shop](/shop) in your [settings](/profile/settings) page!

Thank you for buying something!

## Support

If you're having any issues with your account feel free to [contact us](/contact).
