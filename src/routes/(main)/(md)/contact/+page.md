# Contact Us

How and when to reach out!

## Report Bugs / Make Suggestions

Please report any bugs or make suggestions on the [support Discord server](/support). You may also use the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/EliteFarmers/Website) to report bugs, but the Discord server is preferred.

## Appeal a ban on The Elite Skyblock Farmers

This is not the place to appeal a ban on the Elite Farmers server. The Elite Skyblock Farmers has no
ban appeal system at this time, and trying to contact us about a ban will not result in any action
being taken.

## Questions about farming or Hypixel

You can ask questions about farming or Hypixel in the [community Discord server](/discord). Do not contact us directly with questions like these.

## Shop Issue

Feel free to reach out through the [support Discord server](/support) or [contact@elitenetwork.dev](mailto:contact@elitenetwork.dev) if you have any issues with your purchases!

This can include but is not limited to:

- Not recieving perks
- Gifted items to the wrong user
- Unable to reassign a rejected/expired gift or similar

While you can still reach out, we're less likely to be able help in these scenarios:

- Payment method not supported
- Payment declined

### Creators

If you're a notable person in the Hypixel SkyBlock community and would like a creator code for the Elite Skyblock shop, you can also reach out! While there are no hard requirements at this time, very few exceptions will be made for those who don't have the YouTube rank on Hypixel.

## Legal Contact

For business or legal inquiries, please email [contact@elitenetwork.dev](mailto:contact@elitenetwork.dev).
Any other inquiries may be ignored, and should instead be directed to the [support Discord server](/support).
